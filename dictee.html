✍️ Dictée · listen & write

Dictée

The oldest French drill, and still the best for the ear. You hear a sentence, you type exactly what you heard, and we mark it word by word. It forces you to catch the things you can't quite hear — silent endings, liaisons, and the agreements that betray your level.

💡 Tip: play it twice before typing, use slow for the hard bits, and don't peek. Capitalisation and punctuation don't count — accents and spelling do.

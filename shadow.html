🗣️ Shadowing · listen & repeat

Shadowing

The fastest way to a real accent: hear a phrase and repeat it immediately, copying the rhythm and melody — not waiting until the end. It trains your mouth in prefab chunks, the thing a chatbot can't give you. Record yourself and compare.

💡 The technique: play the model, then speak over it — match the music of the sentence. Do each phrase 3–5 times. For native voices to shadow, use the clips on Watch 🎬.
